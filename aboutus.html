About Milroc

We are Milroc Good Earth, pioneers of the construction and real-estate development business in Goa. Our capacity lies within the will to build not just better homes, but better futures for generations to come. While we design the perfect lifestyle, we seek inspiration from the concept of ‘Home’ with family at its core. Every completed project is a testament to our virtues of uncompromised quality, expertise in craftsmanship, speed, service and undeniable value for money.

Over a period spanning 3 decades, we have cultivated an undisputed leadership in constantly setting new standards within the housing industry. And that, is just the beginning.

The Milroc Philosopy

We believe in the power of good and sustainable construction that designs not only a beautiful lifestyle and residence, but also enables the life and environment around it to flourish. Our capacity lies within the will to build not just better homes, but better futures for generations to come.

WE DO GOOD, TO THE BEST OF OUR CAPABILITIES.

The following are some of the tasks we undertook with the hope and will to support local communities.

Environmental Initiatives

  • We designed and built a Solid Waste Management Unit at Merces for the Merces Village Panchayat, on request from the Merces Vigilant Citizens Association and ‘Chak a Chak Goa’.
  • We conducted a ‘Tree Sapling Plantation’ programme in collaboration with the Corporation of the City of Panaji (CCP) and NGO Chameleon.

Children Development

  • We lent support to the lesser privileged children of Hamara School run by Mrs. Mangala Wagle (in charge) of Kasturba Gandhi National Memorial Trust by way of donations, free supply of uniforms and painting of the school premises.
  • We undertook courtyard development and beautification projects for St. Francis Xavier Academy, Old Goa – for the intellectually disabled and differently abled children.

Community Welfare

  • We are benefactors of the Village Panchayat of Se Old Goa. We have provided garden benches and more than 50 large garbage bins to the village.
  • For the reconstruction and upgradation of the Curca Sub Health Centre, we invested more than 10 lakh rupees in redevelopment.
  • Since almost a decade, we’ve been making monthly contributions to the Panaji Ambulance & Welfare Trust.

Social Partnership

  • We are proud sponsors of the annual events at Fr. Agnel College of Arts & Commerce.
  • Since the past few years, we have also been supporting the annually held Monte Music Festival by Fundaçȃo Oriente.
